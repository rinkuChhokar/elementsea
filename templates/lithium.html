About

Lithium is a chemical element with the symbol Li and atomic number 3. It is a soft, silvery-white alkali metal. Under standard conditions, it is the lightest metal and the lightest solid element. Wikipedia

Group- group 1: hydrogen and alkali metals

Appearance - silvery-white
