About

Hydrogen is the chemical element with the symbol H and atomic number 1. Hydrogen is the lightest element. At standard conditions hydrogen is a gas of diatomic molecules having the formula H₂. It is colorless, odorless, non-toxic, and highly combustible. Wikipedia
