About

Gallium is a chemical element with the symbol Ga and atomic number 31. Discovered by French chemist Paul-Èmile Lecoq De Boisbaudran in 1875, Gallium is in group 13 of the periodic table, and has similarities to the other metals of the group.Wikipedia
